Understanding Estate Planning

Estate planning involves creating a comprehensive plan that outlines how your assets, such as property, investments, and personal belongings, will be managed and distributed after your death. It also includes appointing guardians for minor children, making healthcare decisions, and minimizing potential estate taxes. By working with a qualified estate planning lawyer, you can ensure that your wishes are clearly documented and legally enforceable.
